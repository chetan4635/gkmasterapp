Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 2026

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy outlines how GK Master ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to ensuring your privacy is protected and your data is handled transparently.

2. Information Collection

We collect the following types of information to provide and improve our services:

  • Account Details: Email address, display name, and authentication tokens (via Google or Firebase Auth).
  • Usage Data: Quiz scores, streaks, saved bookmarks, mistaken questions, and earned coins to maintain your leaderboard position and sync progress.
  • Device Information: Basic device and platform data for compatibility and crash reporting.

3. How We Use Information

Collected data is used strictly for core app functionality:

  • Authenticating your account across devices.
  • Generating personalized analytics and adaptive learning paths.
  • Managing the global leaderboard and reward systems.
  • Sending optional push notifications for streaks and new content.

4. Data Security & Third Parties

We utilize industry-standard security measures. We securely integrate with Google Firebase for cloud database hosting (Firestore) and authentication. We neither sell your personal data nor share it with unassociated third-party marketers. Passwords are cryptographically hashed by Firebase and cannot be viewed by our team.

5. Advertising

Our free tier may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may use device identifiers to deliver relevant ads. You can opt out of personalized advertising via your device settings. GK Master Pro subscribers enjoy an ad-free experience.

6. In-App Purchases

GK Master offers an optional premium upgrade ("GK Master Pro") via Google Play's in-app billing. All payment processing is handled securely by Google Play. We do not collect or store your payment card details.

7. User Rights & Data Deletion

You retain full rights to your data. To permanently delete your account and all associated data, submit a request via the Help & Support section in the app or email support@gkmaster.app.

8. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for users aged 13 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with a revised date. Continued use of the app constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact Us

For questions or data deletion requests: support@gkmaster.app
